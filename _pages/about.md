# About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. candidate in the [NLP Group](https://nlp.comp.hkust.edu.hk/) at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://www.ust.hk/), where I am fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/~jxhe/). My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing** and **Machine Learning**, with particular interests in:

- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability

Previously, I graduated from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/english/) with a B.Eng. degree in June 2024.
